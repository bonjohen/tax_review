Methodology (v2.1): Under reform, affected gross LT gains become ordinary income. Corresponding losses are stranded by the $3K/year cap (IRC §1211(b)). Revenue = reform tax on gross gains − current-law tax on net gains. Pipeline: (1) cross-category loss netting, (2) gross gain estimation, (3) holding period filter, (4) bracket-based marginal rate, (5) revenue with stranded losses. Static estimate; does not model behavioral responses.
